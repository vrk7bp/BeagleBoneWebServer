Tim Duncan

Wake Up to Inspiration from Tim Duncan...




“Good, better, best. Never let it rest. Until your good is better and your better is best.”

- Tim Duncan


Inspired by the heroic 2014 NBA Finals win by the San Antonio Spurs. As a way to celebrate the win, and tease my Miami Heat friend, I decided to make an e-mail application that would send a different picture of Tim Duncan (as well as a random quote) to anyone who chose to sign up for the e-mail list. I used the Custom Google Search API and SendGrid API to set this up, and am currently running on an AWS micro instance as a daily cronjob. The code for this application is available on GitHub . Feel free to use it on your own projects.

Insert your e-mail into the text box below and you will be added to the e-mail list. Once added, you will recieve the e-mails every morning.

Note: If you are redirected to the main page of my site, you have signed up correctly. If you are brought back to this Tim Duncan page, then the format of your e-mail address was incorrect.



To unsubscribe, simply put your e-mail into the text box below.
